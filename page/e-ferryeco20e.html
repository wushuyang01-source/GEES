Key Features

In the Stockholm archipelago, a 20-meter ferry now runs on two 125 kW electric motors powered by a NiMH battery, replacing its diesel engine to reduce emissions.

Zero Emission

100% electric propulsion system with zero local emissions, contributing to cleaner waterway environments.

Advanced Control

Precise maneuvering capabilities with General Energies' EMS systems for safe pilot transfers in challenging conditions.

Enhanced Safety

Equipped with two independent systems, the vessel ensures uninterrupted propulsion and safety even if one power source fails.

Classic Design

The entire vessel was designed by Italy's MASS Design Studio, with multiple certifications. It features an elegant exterior and a well-organized interior layout.
