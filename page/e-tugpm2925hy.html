Key Features

PM2925HY is a hybrid propulsion tugboat, providing a solution for the Indonesian market for small-scale new energy tugboats.

Extended Endurance

The vessel adopts a series hybrid system, with the generator providing extended endurance for the ship.

Powerful BP

The tugboat features a strong 25-ton bollard pull, capable of handling various complex port operation demands.

Advanced Control

Precise maneuvering capabilities with General Energies' EMS systems for safe pilot transfers in challenging conditions.

Enhanced Safety

Equipped with two independent systems, the vessel ensures uninterrupted propulsion and safety even if one power source fails.
