VED Product Overview

Reliable Inboard Propulsion Solution

The VED(Variable Electric Drive) is a transmission system suitable for small to medium applications. The device is equipped with Danfoss T200 and T500 motor series. Its core feature lies in the dual-pulley reduction design, which significantly enhances transmission efficiency and stability while reducing operational noise. The VED Electric Vessel Pulley System is not only a model of technological innovation but also lays a solid foundation for the future development of electric vessels.
