Key Features

The PM3040E is a zero-emission tugboat with classic design, excellent maneuverability, smart technology, and dynamic positioning, enabling safe offshore work and supporting the growth of environmentally friendly ports.

Zero Emission

100% electric propulsion system with zero local emissions, contributing to cleaner waterway environments.

Powerful BP

The tugboat features a strong 40-ton bollard pull, capable of handling various complex port operation demands.

Advanced Control

Precise maneuvering capabilities with General Energies' EMS systems for safe pilot transfers in challenging conditions.

Enhanced Safety

Equipped with two independent systems, the vessel ensures uninterrupted propulsion and safety even if one power source fails.
