Product Overview

Reliable Power Management Solution for Your Electrical Systems

The DC-LINK Cabinet is a compact, high-performance power distribution solution designed for small and medium electric ships, supporting DC750V and DC1000V systems. It offers flexible single or dual bus configurations with a bus tie isolator switch, enhancing operational reliability and safety. Integrated DC fuse protection and converter-based overcurrent protection enable efficient fault isolation and selective analysis. The system synchronizes seamlessly with the DC network without additional conditions. Ideal for catamarans, its dual DC bus design maximizes space efficiency while ensuring stable and secure power flow. The DC-LINK Cabinet is optimized for modern marine applications requiring robust, scalable energy management.
