Industry Background

Carbon Neutral Era Opportunities

With the arrival of the carbon neutral era, the marine industry's power systems and ecological logic are undergoing structural transformation. According to State Grid data, by 2050, electric terminal energy consumption will reach 79%-92%, making maritime electrification an irreversible trend.

Trillion-Dollar Blue Ocean Market

By 2050, at least 100,000 medium and large vessels and 4.1 million small inland vessels globally will require electrification retrofits. China's rapid development in electric vehicles and energy storage provides a solid foundation for maritime electrification.

Core Functions

Data Processing & Storage Module

Implements data classification, storage, and permission management, maintaining user project files locally to ensure absolute security of user information

E-Calculator Module

Completes vessel performance and emission calculations, generating detailed analysis reports and intuitive operational interfaces

Data Output Module

The software generates performance reports, single-line diagrams, and simulation results, and visualizes them for the user. Based on the feedback, users adjust design parameters and repeat the optimization process.
