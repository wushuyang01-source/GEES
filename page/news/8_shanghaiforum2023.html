GEES Unveils PM120HY Hybrid Container Ship at Shanghai Electric Boat Innovation Forum

120-container hybrid-powered intermodal transport vessel makes detailed public debut at prestigious industry summit, attracting expert attention from leading maritime institutions

By GEES Forum Team 6 min read
GEES showcases the PM120HY hybrid-powered intermodal container transport ship at the 2023 Electric Boat Industry Innovation Forum in Shanghai, March 30, 2023

Premier Industry Forum Success

The "2023 Electric Boat Industry Innovation and Development Shanghai International Forum and 2023 Ship and Offshore Electrical Technology Industry Development Summit," a focal point of industry attention both domestically and internationally, was successfully held on March 30, 2023, in Shanghai.

This prestigious event gathered domestic and foreign entities to exchange, discuss, and share insights on hot topics such as the development of electric ships in the shipping industry, innovation in electrical systems, application and development of ship battery storage systems, and industry opportunities.

PM120HY Hybrid Container Ship Debut

GEES showcased their innovative new design, the PM120HY hybrid-powered intermodal container transport ship capable of carrying 120 containers on river-sea routes, making its detailed public debut at the forum.

Comprehensive Operational Capability

This innovative vessel is suitable for coastal and inland waterways classified as A and B, demonstrating exceptional versatility for modern container transportation needs.

  • Maritime Transport: Capable of operating from Yangshan Port to Waigaoqiao Terminal for major container operations
  • Urban Waterways: Navigation capability on the Huangpu River for metropolitan cargo transport
  • Lake Operations: Efficient transport across Lake Taihu for regional connectivity
  • Canal Navigation: Beijing-Hangzhou Grand Canal operations connecting major economic centers
  • Regulatory Compliance: Meets national Ministry of Transport standards for inland waterway vessels
