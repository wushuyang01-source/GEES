Introducing the PowerMaster PM120E/HY Series

General Energies proudly presents the PM120E/HY, our flagship electric and hybrid barge solution designed to revolutionize cargo transportation on inland waterways. This innovative vessel combines decades of marine engineering expertise with cutting-edge electric propulsion technology to deliver unmatched efficiency, reliability, and environmental performance.

The PowerMaster series represents our commitment to sustainable maritime logistics, offering both fully electric (PM120E) and hybrid (PM120HY) configurations to meet diverse operational requirements across global inland waterway networks.

Dual Configuration Options

The PowerMaster series offers two distinct propulsion configurations to meet different operational requirements:

PM120E - Full Electric

Zero-emission operation ideal for short to medium range cargo transport with shore charging infrastructure

PM120HY - Hybrid

Extended range capability combining electric propulsion with generator backup for unlimited operational range

Advanced Energy Storage
