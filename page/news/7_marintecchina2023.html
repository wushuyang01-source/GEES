GEES Showcases Comprehensive Electric Vessel Portfolio at Marintec China 2023

Five innovative new energy vessel types displayed at Shanghai's largest maritime exhibition after four-year offline hiatus, attracting global industry attention

By GEES Exhibition Team 6 min read
GEES exhibition booth showcasing five types of new energy vessels at Marintec China 2023, Shanghai, December 5-8, 2023

Historic Return to Offline Exhibition Format

The 2023 Marintec China, held in Shanghai from December 5th to 8th, marked the return of the exhibition to offline format after a four-year hiatus. This edition was hailed as a pivotal event for global maritime industry trends and the largest and most influential professional exhibition in the field, drawing over 2,000 companies from more than 30 countries and regions.

At the exhibition, GEES showcased five types of new energy vessels: E-Pilot, E-Tug, E-Barge, E-CTV, and E-Ferry to domestic and international clients, shipowners, and industry professionals, demonstrating the company's comprehensive approach to maritime electrification.

GEES New Energy Vessel Portfolio

GEES presented a complete ecosystem of electric vessels, each designed to address specific maritime operational requirements while maintaining environmental sustainability and operational efficiency.

Electric pilot boats for efficient harbor navigation and ship guidance services

Electric ferries for passenger transportation and short-distance maritime routes
