Introducing the PMASV38 ECO Series

General Energies unveils the Power Master ASV38 (PMASV38), our latest addition to the ECO Series of hybrid ferries. This advanced vessel represents a breakthrough in sustainable passenger transportation, combining the reliability of traditional ferry operations with the environmental benefits of hybrid propulsion technology.

The PMASV38 is designed to serve coastal and inter-island routes where environmental protection is paramount while maintaining the service reliability that passengers and operators depend on.
