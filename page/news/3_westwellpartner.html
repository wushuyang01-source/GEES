GEES and Westwell Technology Sign Strategic Partnership MoU for Electric Port Transportation

Memorandum of Understanding establishes collaboration framework for developing electric pilot boats, tugboats, and push boats for global port operations

By GEES Corporate Communications 4 min read
Representatives from General Energies Electrical Ship (China) Co., Ltd. and Westwell Technology (Hong Kong) Co., Ltd. at the MoU signing ceremony on August 21, 2024

Strategic Partnership Launch

On August 21, 2024, General Energies Electrical Ship (China) Co., Ltd. and Westwell Technology (Hong Kong) Co., Ltd. officially signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU), marking the beginning of their strategic partnership in the electric port transportation sector.

This collaboration establishes a comprehensive framework for jointly developing and providing innovative electric port transportation solutions, positioning both companies at the forefront of environmentally sustainable port operations worldwide.

Collaboration Scope

According to the signed agreement, both parties will collaborate on developing a comprehensive range of electric port transportation solutions designed to revolutionize port operations globally.

Electric Pilot Boats

Advanced electric propulsion systems for efficient harbor pilotage operations

Electric Tugboats

Powerful electric tugboats for ship assistance and port maneuvering

Electric Push Boats

Specialized electric vessels for barge and cargo transportation

Westwell Technology's Global Presence

Westwell Technology has strategically established its overseas operations headquarters in Hong Kong to support its ambitious international business expansion. The company has demonstrated remarkable success in implementing unmanned electric transport solutions across multiple international markets.

Abu Dhabi Ports

Unmanned Electric Vehicles

Thailand Operations

Port Automation Solutions

Tianjin Port

Electric Transport Systems

Ningbo Port

Automated Port Equipment

Strategic Advantages

This partnership combines GEES's innovative electric vessel technology with Westwell Technology's extensive market experience and technical expertise in port automation and electric transportation.

  • Enhanced Market Position: Strengthened competitive advantage in the global electric port transportation sector
  • Technology Integration: Combining electric vessel expertise with port automation solutions
  • Global Reach: Leveraging Westwell's international presence and GEES's vessel innovation
  • Proven Experience: Building on successful implementations across major international ports
  • Environmental Impact: Advancing green port operations and reducing maritime emissions

Future Outlook

This MoU signals a deepened collaboration between General Energies Electrical Ship (China) Co., Ltd. and Westwell Technology (Hong Kong) Co., Ltd. in the field of electric port transportation technology.

Together, they aim to drive industry development and deliver more innovative and green solutions for port transportation worldwide, establishing new standards for sustainable maritime operations and contributing to global efforts in reducing port-related emissions.

About the Partnership

This strategic alliance combines GEES's cutting-edge electric vessel technology with Westwell Technology's proven expertise in port automation and electric transportation solutions, creating a powerful synergy for advancing global port electrification initiatives.

The partnership leverages both companies' strengths to address the growing demand for sustainable port operations and positions them as leaders in the rapidly evolving electric maritime transportation sector.
