GEES Receives CCS AIP Certification and Signs Strategic MOU at Marintec Shanghai 2023

120 TEU Hybrid Container E-Barge achieves Approval in Principle certification while GEES establishes strategic cooperation framework with CCS Shanghai

By GEES Corporate Communications 5 min read
GEES receives Approval in Principle (AIP) certificate from China Classification Society (CCS) for 120 TEU hybrid container E-Barge and signs strategic MOU at Marintec Shanghai 2023

Dual Milestone Achievement at Marintec Shanghai

During the prestigious 2023 Marintec Shanghai exhibition, General Energies Electric Ship (GEES) achieved two significant milestones that reinforce its position as a leader in sustainable maritime technology innovation.

The company received the Approval in Principle (AIP) certificate from China Classification Society (CCS) for its jointly developed hybrid-powered coastal to river 120 TEU container E-Barge, while simultaneously signing a strategic Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) with CCS Shanghai for comprehensive cooperation in the electrical ship technology field.

120 TEU Hybrid Container E-Barge Certification

The AIP certification covers GEES's revolutionary hybrid-powered coastal to river container vessel, designed to bridge the gap between maritime and inland waterway transportation with unprecedented efficiency and environmental performance.

Strategic MOU with CCS Shanghai

In conjunction with the AIP certification ceremony, GEES signed a comprehensive Memorandum of Understanding with CCS Shanghai, establishing a strategic cooperation framework for advancing electrical ship technology development.

  • Technology Collaboration: Joint development initiatives in electrical ship technology advancement
  • Classification Support: Streamlined certification processes for GEES innovative vessel designs
  • Research Partnership: Collaborative research programs for next-generation maritime technologies
  • Standards Development: Joint contribution to industry standards for electric and hybrid vessels
  • Market Leadership: Combined expertise positioning both organizations as industry leaders

Marintec Shanghai 2023 Significance

The timing of these achievements at Marintec Shanghai 2023, one of Asia's most prestigious maritime exhibitions, underscores the international recognition of GEES's technological leadership in sustainable shipping solutions.

The dual announcement of AIP certification and strategic partnership formalization demonstrates GEES's commitment to advancing the maritime industry's transition toward more sustainable and efficient transportation solutions.

Environmental Impact and Sustainability

The certified hybrid container E-Barge represents a significant advancement in sustainable cargo transportation, offering reduced emissions and improved efficiency compared to conventional vessels operating in coastal and river environments.

  • Emission Reduction: Hybrid propulsion significantly reduces environmental impact in sensitive waterway areas
  • Fuel Efficiency: Optimized power management for enhanced operational economics
  • Noise Reduction: Electric operation modes provide quieter transportation in populated areas
  • Operational Flexibility: Adaptive power systems for varying operational requirements
  • Future-Ready Design: Scalable technology platform for continued innovation
