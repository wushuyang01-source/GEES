Who We Are

General Energies Eletrical Ship (Shanghai) Limited is a pioneering provider of innovative and sustainable energy ship solutions for inland and offshore marine businesses worldwide, based in Shanghai of China. With over 30 years of experience of the founder Kevin Feng, General Energies E-Ship is working with Italy MSS studio to develop all kinds of elegant new energy ship with reliable and cost-effective energy solutions tailored to our clients' unique needs, including E-barge, ferry, CTV, harbor Tug etc.

The team specialize in a wide range of services, including new E-Ship design, onboard DC network system configuration, shallow water azimuth CRP(contra rotating propeller) thruster with PM propulsion motor on the top, swappable Powerbox containerized batteries for charge from shore electricity network, simulation analysis for customer to calculate fuel saving based on battery capacity.
