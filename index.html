Our General Control System (GCS) for E-Ship

We offer an General Control System (GCS) for the E-Ship, which is made up of multiple subsystems

● EMS (Energy Management System)
● PCS (Propulsion Control System)
● SCS (Electric Steering Control System)
● BMS (Battery Management System)
READ MORE

Energy Management System

An integrated system used to monitor, manage, and optimize the energy usage of the vessel for maximum efficiency.

Propulsion Control System

Controls electric motors and propulsion systems with precision, providing smooth operation and optimal thrust management.

Electric Steering Control System

The electric Steering Control System uses servo motors for precise rudder control, eliminating hydraulics, reducing weight, saving energy, and improving efficiency.

Battery Management System

Monitors battery health, temperature, and charging cycles to maximize battery life and ensure safe operation.

More Technologies

Discover our comprehensive range of advanced technologies that power the future of electric vessels

Variable Electric Drive

The Variable Electric Drive (VED) is a dual-pulley electric drive system with Danfoss motors, offering high efficiency, low noise, and stability—paving the way for advanced, reliable electric propulsion in small to medium vessels.

READ MORE

DC-Link Networking

Depend on different type of E-Ships, we choose DC750V or DC1000V as the standard voltage as our standard DC-Link networking, with single-segment or double-segment DC bus section operation, connected to the Battery Energy Storage System(BESS) or the V.S Genset.

READ MORE

Variable Hybrid Drive

The Variable Hybrid Drive (VHD) is a compact hybrid drive system designed for easy marine installation, offering excellent noise reduction, reliability, and optimal performance with low-torque e-machines through 2.5:1 or 3:1 gearing.

About Us

GEES is a pioneering provider of innovative and sustainable energy ship solutions for inland and offshore marine businesses worldwide, based in Shanghai of China, with over 30 years of experience of the founder Kevin Feng. GEES is working with Italy MSS studio to develop all kinds of elegant new energy ship with reliable and cost-effective energy solutions tailored to out clients unique needs, including E-Barge, E-Ferry, E-CTV, E-Tug and E-Pilot.
